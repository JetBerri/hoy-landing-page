Frequently Asked Questions

General Information

1. What does the ticket price include?

Your ticket includes:

  • Free transportation (pickup and drop-off from a specific location).
  • Tapas on arrival.
  • Unlimited beer, sangria, water, and non-alcoholic beverages.
  • Access to the villa and entertainment.
2. Where is the event taking place?

The villa is located in loriguilla (Valencia countryside). The exact address will be shared with confirmed guests.

3. What time does the event start and end?
  • Pickup time: 19.30
  • Event duration: 20.00 - 04.00
  • Drop-off time: 4.10
4. How do I get to the pickup location?

We will provide a central meeting point in Valencia, easily accessible by public transport and taxis.

Food & Drinks

5. What food is included?

Tapas will be served upon arrival. Additional meals will be available for purchase, priced between €5-10.

6. What drinks are included?

Unlimited beer, sangria, water, and soft drinks.

7. Can I bring my own alcohol?

No outside alcohol is allowed. However, stronger liquor (vodka, whiskey, rum, etc.) will be available for purchase at €5 per drink.

Tickets & Payment

8. How do I buy a ticket?

Tickets can be purchased via bank transfer, or through a promoter.

9. Are refunds available?

Tickets are non-refundable but can be transferred to another person.

10. Can I pay at the door?

No, all tickets must be purchased in advance to secure your spot.

Rules & Policies

11. Is there a dress code?

Casual and comfortable, but dress to impress.

12. Are there age restrictions?

The event is 18+. ID checks may be required.

13. Is there a security check?

Yes, for safety reasons, all guests will be checked at the entrance.

14. Can I leave and come back?

There is enough space to walk around the compound. Leaving the building would be restricted as drinking is not allowed on the streets.

Other Questions

16. Can I bring a plus-one?

All guests must have been allocated a number before the event. Limited slots available, so book early!

17. What happens if I miss the pickup?

We recommend arriving early, as transportation is scheduled. If you miss the pickup, you will need to arrange your own transport. Your space on the bus will still be reserved to take you back to Valencia if need be.

Indoor & Outdoor Party

17. Can we stay outside after 10 PM?

Yes, you can stay outdoors to relax and socialize, but music will be turned off after 10 PM.

18. Will there still be music and entertainment after 10 PM?

Yes! The indoor party will continue with music, dancing, and entertainment until 4 AM.

19. Can I go back and forth between indoor and outdoor areas?

Yes, but all loud activities, including music and dancing, will take place indoors after 10 PM.

22. Will there be a limit on alcohol consumption?

Guests can enjoy unlimited beer, sangria, water, and non-alcoholic beverages. However, responsible drinking is encouraged, and staff may refuse service if necessary.

23. What happens if a guest appears too intoxicated?

For safety reasons, guests showing clear signs of intoxication will not be served more alcohol. Security or event staff may intervene if necessary to ensure a safe environment for everyone.
